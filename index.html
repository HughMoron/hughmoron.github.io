My way to and with the DCI...

On the 15th of April 2019 I joined the DCI for a four week orientation class where we learned our first steps at HTML, CSS and Java. Also we had several workshops for Scrum, Self-Learning and even Robotics.
When we weren't at DCI learing Web Development we were on company tours getting first impressions of how our future work life could look like.
All in all those were very interesting weeks full of learning and I am looking forward to joining the 1-year course and continue my journey to become a succesfull web developer.

Ein riesengroßes Dankeschön gebührt dabei auch dem INGA Team Mitte und insbesondere Frau Hohlfeldt, die mir die Chance erst ermöglicht haben, dass ich dieses spannende Feld weiter erforschen und meine Zukunft darin finden kann.
